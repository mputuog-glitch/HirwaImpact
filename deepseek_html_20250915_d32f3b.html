About HirwaImpact

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty by providing holistic support to vulnerable children and their families

HirwaImpact exists to secure a prosperous future for vulnerable children by providing for their essential needs and empowering their families to thrive. The word "Hirwa" (meaning "Green" or "Prosperity" in Kinyarwanda) reflects our commitment to growth and bright futures.

We believe every child deserves a foundation for a prosperous future, which is why we ensure access to education, shelter, medical care, and clothing, while also empowering their parents with the tools to build a stable, self-sufficient home.

Our unique, two-generation model supports both the child and the parent, creating a stable foundation for lasting change and breaking the cycle of poverty for generations to come.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our core principles, we work towards a future where every child can thrive

Our Mission

To nurture the holistic development of every child in our care. We provide immediate essentials—a safe home, medical care, education, and clothing—while simultaneously working with their families to create environments of stability and self-sufficiency.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child, regardless of their origin, has the opportunity to grow, learn, and prosper in a safe and loving environment. A future where communities are strengthened, and families are empowered to be the primary architects of their own bright tomorrow.

Our Founder

Founder's Image

NDIZIHIWE HIRWA WESLEY

Born into a devout Christian family in Rwanda, NDIZIHIWE HIRWA WESLEY developed a deep compassion for vulnerable children from an early age. Growing up, he witnessed firsthand the struggles faced by children in his community who lacked access to basic necessities like education, shelter, and healthcare.

His Christian upbringing instilled in him the values of service, compassion, and the belief that every child is created with purpose and potential. After completing his education, Wesley felt a divine calling to address the systemic issues keeping children in poverty.

"Every child deserves the opportunity to fulfill their God-given potential. It is our privilege to walk alongside them, removing barriers and nurturing dreams."

In 2022, after years of volunteering with local community organizations, Wesley established HirwaImpact with a vision to create holistic change. The name "Hirwa" meaning "Prosperity" in Kinyarwanda, reflects his commitment to helping children and families not just survive, but thrive.

Under Wesley's leadership, HirwaImpact has grown from a small initiative helping a handful of children to an organization making significant impact across multiple communities. His unique approach of supporting both children and their parents has proven effective in creating sustainable change.
